Our Approach

Security

We begin with network and corporate discovery. By reviewing your threat landscape and business objectives, we define a tiered security strategy and structure value-driven projects aligned to corporate OKRs and risk-based priorities.

  • Threat modeling & risk assessment (NIST CSF, ISO 27005)
  • Architecture baselines: SSE/SASE/Zero-Trust, identity & MFA
  • Data protection strategy: CASB/DLP, email security, phishing resistance

Visibility

We explore your attack surface: processes, assets, identities, vendors, and data flows. We map unidentified risks, validate control effectiveness, and expose shadow IT. Results are translated to C-level language that reflects risk appetite & tolerance.

  • Attack Surface Management (ASM) & asset/identity inventory
  • Telemetry depth: SIEM/XDR detections, logging coverage & quality
  • Risky & sanctioned apps: exception workflow, CASB/DLP insights

Administration

Administration is persistence and sustained success. We embed governance, ownership, change management, and continuous improvement so outcomes last beyond projects. Every service is value-oriented and leaves a notable transformation.

  • Governance: policies/standards, exception management, steering cadence
  • Operating model: RACI, service catalogue, intake & prioritization
  • Metrics & audits: OKRs, KRIs, MTTR/MTTD, post-incident reviews

About Violet Bridge Security

Violet Bridge Security was founded on the belief that true cybersecurity excellence is built on knowledge, experience, and culture. Our foundation comes from experienced and talented cybersecurity professionals, alumni of the Master of Science in Cybersecurity program at New York University (NYU). We take pride in our expertise and the research contributions made at the NYU Center for Cybersecurity (CCS).

Over the past decade, our team has faced challenges, made sacrifices, and achieved milestones while working with global teams on complex cybersecurity projects across diverse industries. These experiences shaped not only our technical proficiency but also our commitment to building secure, sustainable, and people-centric digital environments.

The name Violet Bridge Security pays homage to our roots in New York. The Brooklyn Bridge, one of the city's most iconic landmarks since its opening in 1883, symbolizes resilience, connection, and innovation. Its suspension cables—designed by NYU Polytechnic students—reflect the same spirit of engineering and vision that drives our work today. And if there is one color that represents both the Brooklyn Bridge and our alma mater, it is violet, NYU's defining color.

The NYU Tandon School of Engineering, with origins dating back to 1854, embodies the principle that "you can be born anywhere, but made right here in Brooklyn." Violet Bridge Security carries that same spirit forward: connecting global expertise with local challenges, bridging innovation with practicality, and transforming cybersecurity from a technical barrier into a strategic advantage for the organizations we serve.

At Violet Bridge Security, we don't just protect systems—we empower organizations to thrive with confidence in a digital-first world.
